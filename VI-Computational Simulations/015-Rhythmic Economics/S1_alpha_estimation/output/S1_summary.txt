S1: Estimating Economic Coherence Exponent (α)
==============================================
Date: 2026-02-14 18:06:49

RTM-ECON MODEL
--------------
τ(L) = τ_0 × (L/L_ref)^α

where:
  τ = characteristic time (recovery, persistence)
  L = scale proxy (market cap, firm size)
  α = coherence exponent

INTERPRETATION OF α
-------------------
α > 0.4: High coherence (buffered, staged decisions)
α ~ 0.3-0.4: Moderate coherence
α < 0.3: Low coherence (cascade risk, rapid propagation)

MARKET REGIME PARAMETERS
------------------------
Stable Growth (2004-2006): α = 0.45
  Normal market conditions, good coherence
Pre-Crisis (2007): α = 0.35
  Coherence declining, compression
Crisis (2008-2009): α = 0.20
  Decoherence, rapid cascade potential
Recovery (2010-2012): α = 0.40
  Rebuilding coherence
New Normal (2013-2019): α = 0.42
  Post-crisis stable regime

ESTIMATION RESULTS
------------------
Stable Growth: True α = 0.45, Est α = 0.456 [0.443, 0.470]
Pre-Crisis: True α = 0.35, Est α = 0.356 [0.343, 0.370]

Mean absolute error: 0.0056

META-ANALYSIS (Stable Growth, 4 families)
-----------------------------------------
Combined ECI: 0.447
95% CI: [0.348, 0.546]
Heterogeneity I²: 0.00
True α: 0.42

METHODOLOGY
-----------
1. Group firms by market cap tiers
2. Measure characteristic times per tier
3. Fit log(τ) vs log(market cap) → slope = α
4. Use Theil-Sen for robustness to outliers
5. Meta-analyze across proxy families
