## 2. The Civil Engineering Challenge

### 2.1 Forces That Destroy Infrastructure

| Force | Effect | Current Solution |

More mass = more cost, more foundation, more environmental impact.

## 3. RTM Principles Applied to Structures

### 3.1 Topological Load Management

Alpha-gradient regions redirect mechanical stress to optimal paths, allowing targeted reinforcement instead of uniform strength.

### 3.2 Seismic Decoupling

From SEISMOLOGY_SPINOFFS: alpha-gradients affect wave propagation. Seismic waves bend around foundation. Building experiences minimal shaking. No mechanical bearings (fail-proof).

### 3.3 Self-Sensing Structures

Topological fibers (from SMART_TEXTILES) embedded in concrete: every fiber senses strain, whole structure becomes sensor, real-time damage detection.
