        g = gravitational acceleration
        ρ_p = particle density

        ρ_Al = 2400 kg/m³

    v = 4.6 mm/s
    
    In 60 seconds: 27 cm of separation
    
    COMPLETE SEGREGATION IS INEVITABLE.


UNDER TOPOLOGICAL STASIS (g → 0):

    v = (2r²×0×(ρ_p - ρ_f)) / (9η)
    v = 0
    
    NO SEPARATION OCCURS.
    
    Atoms remain homogeneously distributed.
    Metallic bonds form between unlike atoms.
    Result: TRUE ALLOY.
