## 3. RTM Principles Applied to Textiles

### 3.1 Topological Fibers

Individual fibers incorporating α-gradient metamaterial core:
- Diameter: 50-100 µm (same as synthetic fibers)
- Core: α-gradient metamaterial
- Sheath: Piezo-responsive coating
- Outer: Protective polymer

### 3.2 Response Mechanisms

| Stimulus | α Response | Effect |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Strain | α increases | Stiffening (protection) |
| Temperature | α shifts | Conductivity change |
| Vibration | α oscillates | Energy harvesting |
| Pressure | α gradient | Sensing signal |
