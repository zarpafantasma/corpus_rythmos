## 12. Mathematical Framework

### 12.1 Thrust Equation

    F = V × κ × (∇α)³

    Where:
        F = thrust (N)
        V = core volume (m³)
        κ = vacuum coupling (~10⁻¹² N/m³ per unit gradient cubed)
        ∇α = topological gradient (m⁻¹)

### 12.2 Power-Thrust Relationship

    P = η × F × c_eff

    Where:
        P = electrical power (W)
        η = conversion efficiency (~0.1-0.5)
        F = thrust (N)
        c_eff = effective "exhaust" velocity (relates to α gradient)

### 12.3 Mission Δv

Without propellant mass:

    Δv = (F/m) × t = a × t

    Limited only by power duration and structural acceleration limits.
