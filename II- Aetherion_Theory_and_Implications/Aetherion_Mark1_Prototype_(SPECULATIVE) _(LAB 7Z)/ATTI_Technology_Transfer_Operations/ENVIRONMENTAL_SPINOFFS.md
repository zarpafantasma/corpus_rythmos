## 11. Mathematical Framework

### 11.1 Selective Transport Equation

Flux of species i in α-gradient:

    J_i = -D_i × ∇c_i + v_α,i × c_i

Where:
- D_i = diffusion coefficient
- c_i = concentration
- v_α,i = α-driven velocity (species-specific)

Selectivity:

    S_ij = v_α,i / v_α,j

For CO₂ vs N₂: S > 100 (highly selective)

### 11.2 Energy Requirements

Minimum separation energy:

    E_min = RT × Σ x_i × ln(x_i)

RTM approaches minimum through passive topological transport.
